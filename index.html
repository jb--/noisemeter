About Noise Meter

Noise meter is designed to measure and monitor noise levels in your environment. If you cross a threshold, you'd hear a sound. Since every devices microphone might be slightly different, it's quite a tough job to get accurate decibel readings. Here, we circumvent the issue by not even trying to give you a number. Read about A-weighting for more information. You can use the sensitivity slider to adjust the threshold at which the alarm goes off.

Privacy

This app runs entirely in your browser. Your microphone data is not sent to any server. I do not store any of your data on my servers.
